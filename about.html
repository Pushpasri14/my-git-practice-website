About Apex AI Scanner

A cinematic interface for multimodal perception. The demo showcases a 4K portrait with scanning HUD overlays and simulated metrics for primary emotions, gaze direction, and sustained eye turns. It’s designed as a front-end shell to connect with your Python video analysis pipeline.

To integrate with your backend later, expose an endpoint that streams analysis updates (emotion, gaze, eyeTurnCount), then replace the simulated updates in app.js with real-time data.
